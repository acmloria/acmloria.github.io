About Me

Greetings! I’m Angelica Cassandra Loria, a passionate data enthusiast with a flair for languages and a drive to innovate. With a master's degree in International Masters Program in Information Systems and Applications from National Tsing Hua University in Taiwan, I embarked on a journey that seamlessly integrated my love for data science and my fascination with languages.

During my time at National Tsing Hua University, I was honored to receive the ICDF scholarship, which not only facilitated my education but also introduced me to the vibrant world of Mandarin Chinese. What began as a requirement soon evolved into a genuine interest, leading me to explore other languages like Spanish and Japanese. I've always believed that the ability to communicate in different tongues opens doors to diverse perspectives and opportunities.

My academic journey was intertwined with numerous data science projects, where I delved into the intricacies of data modeling and analysis. My master's thesis focused on Natural Language Processing (NLP), a field that captivated me with its potential to revolutionize how we interact with data. I discovered that the power of data extends far beyond numbers and statistics; it has the ability to detect fraud, predict diseases, and mitigate losses for businesses, among countless other applications.

  • Language Learner: I’m on a perpetual quest to become a polyglot, albeit with some frustration! Mandarin, Spanish, Japanese and Sign Language are among the languages I’m currently learning.
